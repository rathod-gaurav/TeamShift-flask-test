“Precision farming” or “Technology enabled approach to farming” uses data of the system continuously to analyse and provide information on crop growth patterns, and give actionable timely insights to prevent losses and optimize productivity.

Our Motivation
Hydroponics uses 10% of the water required as compared to traditional agriculture. Also, fertile, arable land is in scarce supply especially as the population is expected to increase by 50% in the next 20 years.

By eliminating the need for land, hydroponics can serve as a major food source in coming years, as it gives scalable produce in less amount of time. But It also requisites precision techniques and knowledge about Hydroponics which is not achievable for a common man.

So, we aim at developing a fully autonomous system capable of managing the parameters accurately, with user-friendly insights. Hence, the goal is to increase productivity in a sustainable and cost-efficient way.
